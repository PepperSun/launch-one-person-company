# Tool Substitutes

Default to lean, low-cost, low-complexity tools. Show substitutes so the user understands trade-offs and upgrade paths.

## Recommendation Format

```markdown
| Category | Lean Default | Standard Substitute | Pro / Regulated Substitute | When To Upgrade |
|---|---|---|---|---|
```

For each important tool choice, include advantages, disadvantages, approximate cost, and migration triggers.

## Categories

| Category | Lean Default | Standard Substitute | Pro / Regulated Substitute | When To Upgrade |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Domain | Existing registrar, Cloudflare Registrar, Porkbun, or Namecheap | Squarespace Domains or another mainstream registrar | Managed IT provider | Brand matters or DNS setup becomes complex |
| Email | Google Workspace starter or Zoho Mail | Google Workspace Business, Microsoft 365 | Managed compliance email | Sending volume, team growth, or enterprise customers |
| Website | No site, Carrd, Notion page, or static HTML | Framer, Webflow, Squarespace | Custom app or CMS | Paid acquisition, SEO, integrations, or enterprise trust |
| CRM | Google Sheets or Airtable | HubSpot free or Pipedrive | Salesforce or regulated CRM | More than 30 active leads or multiple stages/users |
| Payment | Manual invoice or Stripe Payment Links | Stripe Billing, Paddle, Lemon Squeezy | Merchant account or enterprise billing | Recurring billing, global tax handling, or high volume |
| Accounting | Spreadsheet plus receipt folder | QuickBooks, Xero, Wave | CPA-managed bookkeeping | Recurring revenue, contractors, sales tax, or S-corp review |
| Contracts | Template plus e-signature | Dropbox Sign, DocuSign, PandaDoc | Attorney-reviewed contract system | High-value contracts or regulated work |
| Support | Dedicated email inbox | Help Scout, Zendesk, Intercom | Enterprise helpdesk | More than a few support requests per week |
| Analytics | Plausible, Fathom, PostHog free, or simple server logs | GA4, PostHog paid, Mixpanel | Enterprise analytics with governance | Product-led growth or enterprise reporting |
| Scheduling | Calendly free, Google appointment schedule | Calendly paid, SavvyCal | Sales scheduling platform | Qualification routing or team handoff |
| Social | One founder account and selected communities | Founder plus brand account | Community manager or paid social workflow | Clear positioning and repeatable content motion |
| AI Provider | One primary API or hosted model | Multi-provider abstraction | Enterprise agreement or private deployment | Sensitive data, reliability, or customer procurement demands |

## Guardrails

- Do not recommend a paid stack before validation unless the risk or customer requirement justifies it.
- Do not recommend regulated or enterprise tools as credibility decoration.
- Prefer tools the solo founder can operate alone.
- Include cancellation or migration notes for tools with lock-in.
